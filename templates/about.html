Who We Are

Zeed is a forward-thinking social ecosystem designed to prioritize audio communication. Operated by a global, remote-first development collective with our core operational hub in Tanzania, we work to build digital spaces that transcend regional borders. We aim to reshape digital connection by creating spaces where human expression relies on vocal timber rather than calculated imagery.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build an inclusive digital sanctuary that celebrates authenticity. The human voice carries raw emotional nuances, truth, and depth that plain text files and filtered photographs fail to convey. Zeed empowers thinkers, artists, and creators to build real communities using real conversations.

What We Do

Zeed provides an ecosystem designed for high-fidelity audio sharing, real-time audio calls, and cross-border social feeds. Our software scales performance metrics intelligently to keep latency to a minimum, ensuring your audio distributions and live connections remain stable across varying network environments.

Data Ethics and Transparency

In compliance with strict international user-protection frameworks and Google Play guidelines, Zeed stands by an absolute promise: your private audio uploads, voice call details, and registration identifiers are never compiled, packaged, or sold to third-party data analytics firms. Your profile workspace is secure, and your creative output belongs entirely to you.
