Who We Are

We are Plugbar LLC, a company incorporated in Delaware, USA, with a remote-first team and headquarters operating from Tanzania. We are building a new kind of social platform centered around audio — where voices matter more than visuals.

Our Mission

To create a digital sanctuary where authenticity thrives. We believe the human voice carries emotions that text and images simply cannot capture.

What We Do

We are the first audio-focused social platform designed for real engagement. Share stories, interact with listeners, and discover human connection.

Why Audio?

In a world of filtered photos and short-lived videos, audio brings back authenticity. It allows you to connect emotionally, multitask, and engage in a deeply personal way.
