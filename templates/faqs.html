Everything you need to know about plugbar

How does the audio visualizer work?
▼
Our dynamic visualizer analyzes the audio frequencies in real-time as you play a track. The green bars will bounce and dance according to the beat of the music or voice recording!
Who can see my audio posts?
▼
By default, your posts are visible to everyone on the feed. You can gain followers who will see your latest audio stories directly on their home page.
What audio formats are supported?
▼
We currently support MP3, WAV, and M4A formats. For the best experience, we recommend uploading high-quality MP3 files under 20MB.
How do I get my posts trending?
▼
Posts with more "Likes" and "Comments" move higher up the global feed. Engaging with other creators is the best way to grow your audience!
I'm seeing a "No such column" error. What do I do?
▼
This usually happens during our platform updates. Try logging out and back in, or contact support if the issue persists in your profile settings.
