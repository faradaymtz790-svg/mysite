Frequently Asked System Queries

How are audio files and metrics structured on the feed?
Zeed parses standard format audio uploads dynamically. Post files map length attributes directly into stream states, allowing symmetric synchronization across followers instantly via optimized backend architecture.
My live WebRTC audio call dropped. How do I fix it?
Voice connections operate over low-latency peer endpoints. If a call drops, check your local data connection metrics. The application automatically re-initializes clean handshakes within 5 seconds if signals drop.
How do I permanently delete my account profile files?
Navigate directly to your account control configurations page. Choosing permanent deletion flushes your database entries, audio paths, and activity keys across our servers within 24 hours.
