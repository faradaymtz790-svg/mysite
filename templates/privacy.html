Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Welcome to Plugbar. We believe that your creative audio journey deserves a safe and private environment. This policy outlines how we handle your data when you interact with our platform.

1. The Data We Value

To provide you with the best audio social experience, we process two types of data:

  • Identity Data: Your username, email, and security credentials.
  • Creative Data: The audio stories you record, the cover images you upload, and your profile metadata.

2. Our Commitment

plugbar is built for creators. We promise to never sell your private audio files or personal contact details to third-party marketing firms. Your content belongs to you.

We use your data strictly to improve our recommendation algorithms, ensure the stability of our media servers, and maintain a safe, spam-free community.

3. Public vs. Private

When you post an audio story, it is shared with the public feed so your community can engage with it. However, your email address, password, and internal account settings remain strictly private and accessible only to you.

4. Your Control

You remain the pilot of your Togsphere experience. At any time, you can:

  • Modify your profile information in your settings.
  • Remove any post, which triggers an immediate deletion of that file from our servers.
  • Request full account deactivation by contacting our support team.

5. Security

We employ modern encryption standards to protect your data. While we strive to keep our platform secure, we recommend that you use a unique password and keep your login details protected.

6. Questions?

If you have any concerns regarding how your data is handled, our team is ready to assist. Please visit our Help Center to open a support ticket.
